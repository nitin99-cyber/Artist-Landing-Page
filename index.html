The Beginning

Born Into Music

In the heart of Murshidabad, beside the tranquil Bhagirathi river, a boy was born whose voice would one day make a billion people cry, smile, and fall in love — all at once.

Raised in a family steeped in music traditions, Arijit began rigorous training in Indian Classical music at a young age. Influenced by family musicians and the raw emotions of rural Bengal, he absorbed the soulfulness that would later define his signature sound.

He was shaped not by glam and spotlight, but by riyaz (practice), patience, and an unquenchable passion for honest musical expression.

His Path

Career Journey

2005

Fame Gurukul

Participated in the national singing reality show Fame Gurukul — the spark that ignited a legendary journey.

2011

Bollywood Debut

Phir Mohabbat — his first Bollywood appearance, a preview of the voice that would conquer hearts.

2013

Breakthrough Moment

Tum Hi Ho from Aashiqui 2 became a cultural phenomenon — India stopped and listened.

2018

National Award

Binte Dil earns him the National Film Award — recognition of his unmatched artistry.

2022

National Award

Kesariya — again honoured at the National Awards, reaffirming his dominant era.

2025

Padma Shri 🏆

India's fourth-highest civilian honour — awarded the Padma Shri for his extraordinary contribution to music and culture.
